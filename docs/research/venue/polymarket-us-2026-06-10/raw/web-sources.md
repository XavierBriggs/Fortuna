## CFTC — DCO (clearing)

- QC Clearing LLC d/b/a **Polymarket Clearing** is a registered Derivatives
  Clearing Organization. CFTC DCO filing page:
  https://www.cftc.gov/IndustryOversight/IndustryFilings/ClearingOrganizations/50836
- Rule filing letterhead: "QC Clearing LLC, d/b/a Polymarket Clearing, 7251 W.
  Palmetto Park Road" — https://www.cftc.gov/sites/default/files/filings/orgrules/25/09/rules09092530154.pdf
- Search summary (CFTC order): "registered by Commission order; permitted to
  clear fully collateralized positions in futures contracts, options on futures
  contracts, and swaps … clears only fully collateralized positions and does not
  employ a margin-setting methodology."
- CFTC No-Action Letter 25-48 (Dec 11, 2025) references QCX LLC / QC Clearing:
  https://www.cftc.gov/csl/25-48/download
