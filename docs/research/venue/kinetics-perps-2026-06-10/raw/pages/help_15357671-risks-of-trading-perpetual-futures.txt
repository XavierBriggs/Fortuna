Liquidation risk
If the market moves against your position and your account balance falls below the maintenance margin threshold, Kalshi Klear will liquidate your position. This can happen quickly in volatile markets. At 6x leverage, a ~17% adverse price move can wipe out your entire margin. Set a stop-loss to help manage this risk.
Leverage risk
Leverage amplifies gains but also amplifies losses by the same factor. A position that appears profitable can be eliminated by a relatively small adverse price move. Always understand your liquidation price before entering a leveraged trade.
Funding rate costs
Funding payments occur every 8 hours. If you hold a leveraged position for an extended period, these costs can accumulate — even if the price moves in your favor. Always factor funding into your cost of holding a position.
Market volatility
Crypto markets can move sharply and without warning. A sudden price swing can trigger liquidation faster than you can react. Use stop-losses, size your positions appropriately, and only trade amounts you can afford to lose.
Operational and infrastructure risk
Kalshi is a CFTC-regulated exchange. However, all trading platforms are subject to technical issues, outages, or unforeseen events. Kalshi performs scheduled maintenance on Thursdays from approximately 3:00–5:00 AM ET. During unscheduled outages, orders may not be executable.
Regulatory risk
Perpetual futures are a regulated financial product. Regulatory changes could affect the availability of the product, margin requirements, leverage limits, or other aspects of trading.
