Affiliation with KalshiEx LLC and Kalshi Klear LLC
Kinetic Markets, LLC (the “Firm”) is a futures commission merchant (“FCM”) that is wholly owned by
Kalshi Inc. Kalshi Inc. also owns KalshiEx LLC (“KalshiEx”), a board of trade designated by the
Commodity Futures Trading Commission (the “CFTC”) as a contract market, and Kalshi Klear LLC
(“Kalshi Klear”), a derivatives clearing organization registered with the CFTC.
Because of this common ownership, Kinetic Markets is an affiliate of KalshiEx and Kalshi Klear, and
certain conflicts of interest may exist.
In particular:
●

The Firm may act as an FCM for customer transactions executed on KalshiEx and cleared
through Kalshi Klear, each of which is an affiliate of the Firm.

●

Kalshi Inc. and its affiliates may have overlapping financial interests in the operation and success
of KalshiEx, Kalshi Klear, and the Firm.

●

Certain officers or employees may serve in roles across affiliated entities.

KalshiEx and Kalshi Klear apply their rules, margin requirements, risk controls, and disciplinary processes
to all participants in accordance with their respective rulebooks. The Firm is subject to the same rules and
regulatory requirements as any other FCM interacting with KalshiEx or Kalshi Klear. Further, KalshiEx,
Kalshi Klear and the Firm maintain policies and procedures designed to mitigate conflicts of interest
arising from their affiliate relationships.
Customers should consider these relationships and potential conflicts of interest when deciding whether
to open or maintain an account with the Firm.
