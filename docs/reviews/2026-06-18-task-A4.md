## Findings

- [Minor] Docstring lie: `fills_aggregate` docstring says "the daemon skips insert when
  n_fills is 0" but the daemon does not guard on `agg.n_fills > 0`. In standard
  Postgres, UNIQUE(market_id, strategy) with strategy=NULL does not prevent duplicate
  insertions (NULL != NULL in unique constraints), so a market settling with no fills
  could accumulate multiple `trade_scores` rows under crash-and-replay. The brief does
  not mandate this guard in the daemon logic (brief line 32 makes no mention of a skip);
  the promise is only in the docstring. In the demo scope, paper-on-live markets always
  have fills before settling, so no operational defect today. However, the docstring is
  a false contract. Requires: either (a) delete the docstring claim, or (b) add the
  `if agg.n_fills > 0` guard in the daemon and a corresponding GAPS.md entry. Not a
  Critical (no money-path invariant violated; I5 append-only is for audit rows, not this
  calibration substrate; fills pool is the same path). File: `repos.rs:279-280`.
